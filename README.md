HealthConnect is a state of the art healthcare application that connects 4 different parties: Patients, Doctors, Pharmacies, and Hospitals. The application uses JavaFx as the front-end with a Google Firebase back-end. The map feature uses Leaflet and Open Street Maps using an HTML file with Javascript to load a map to view locations of each healthcare provider. Every healthcare provider would need to register an account to offer their services and claim a map location so patients know where they are located. Each type of account has their own dashboard when they login in and each account can do a variety of things.

What Each Account Can Do

Patients create/update their profiles, find doctors, schedule appointments, view their prescriptions, browse hospital locations, view medical reports, message doctors and pharmacies regarding visits and medications, as well as receiving notifications regarding their appointments, medications, hospital visits, and diagnostic results.

Doctors create/update their profiles, view a list of their patients, edit patient profiles for diagnosis, message patients and pharmacies, view their daily/weekly schedule where they can send prescriptions, complete appointments with summaries on what occurred and why the patient visited, view medical reports, and notifications for appointments and hospital results. Doctors also register for hospital departments and refer their patients for hospital visits.

Pharmacies receive prescriptions sent by doctors and hospitals, they can view patient profiles to verify insurance, view allergy, current medication, chronic conditions, medical history, and past appointments, search for prescriptions by patient or medication name and see the dosage and refill information, message patients and doctors, mark the prescriptions as ready and confirm that the patient picked up the medication.

Hospitals view patient lists, patient profiles as well as their daily/weekly schedule when doctors refer patients for a hospital visit with options to reschedule or cancel if needed, they can also send prescriptions to pharmacies, view all medical reports for patients that previously visited that hospital, upload diagnostic results/medical reports and hospital findings to be seen by the patient and doctor after the visit and view which doctors are registered for each department in the hospital.
